Sakura Temple

Our Sakura Temple is like the main hub in our civilisation. In here, you can find a class, toliets, a hospital, and SpeedFood — our local fast food chain. You can also find his/her majesty's throne.

Village

The village houses of Temple Town are handbuilt in the past decade, therefore having advanced earthquake protection and technology. Each house for living consists of a dining room, a toliet, and a kitchen. There is also the house of the scientist, which has a mysterious portal.

Docks

The docks are one of the most important places in our town. This is because the best-selling dish at SpeedFood is deep-fried cod. You can ride a boat through the cave, or up/down the boat ramp. The local fisherman — Bernard Hackwell — lives here at the docks in his nice little house.
